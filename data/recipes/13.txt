
Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

    8 oz pasta
    2 cups fresh basil leaves
    1/3 cup pine nuts
    2 cloves garlic
    1/2 cup olive oil
    1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    Salt to taste

Instructions:

    Cook pasta according to package instructions.
    Blend basil, pine nuts, garlic, and olive oil in a food processor.
    Stir in Parmesan cheese and salt.
    Toss cooked pasta with pesto sauce.
    Serve warm with extra Parmesan if desired.
