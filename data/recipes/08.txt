Easy Baked Salmon

Ingredients:

    4 salmon fillets
    2 tbsp olive oil
    1 lemon, sliced
    2 cloves garlic, minced
    1 tsp dried dill
    Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

    Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
    Place salmon in a baking dish, brush with olive oil.
    Top with lemon slices, garlic, and dill.
    Season with salt and pepper.
    Bake for 12-15 minutes until salmon flakes easily.
