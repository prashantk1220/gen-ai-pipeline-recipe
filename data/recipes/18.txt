
Shrimp Scampi

Ingredients:

    1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
    8 oz linguine pasta
    4 tbsp butter
    4 cloves garlic, minced
    1/4 cup white wine
    2 tbsp lemon juice
    1/4 cup chopped parsley
    Salt and red pepper flakes to taste

Instructions:

    Cook pasta according to package instructions.
    Melt butter in a large skillet, add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    Add shrimp, cook for 2-3 minutes per side until pink.
    Pour in wine and lemon juice, simmer for 2 minutes.
    Toss with pasta, add parsley, salt, and red pepper flakes.
