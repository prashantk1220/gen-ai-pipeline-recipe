
Simple Guacamole

Ingredients:

    2 ripe avocados
    1 small onion, finely chopped
    1 tomato, diced
    1 lime, juiced
    Salt to taste
    Optional: 1 jalapeño, minced

Instructions:

    Mash avocados in a bowl.
    Mix in onion, tomato, and jalapeño (if using).
    Add lime juice and salt, stir well.
    Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
    Serve with tortilla chips.
