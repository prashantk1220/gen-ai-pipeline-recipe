
Beef Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

    1 lb beef sirloin, sliced thin
    2 cups mixed vegetables
    3 tbsp soy sauce
    1 tbsp cornstarch
    2 tbsp vegetable oil
    2 cloves garlic, minced
    1 tsp grated ginger

Instructions:

    Mix soy sauce and cornstarch, coat beef slices.
    Heat oil in a wok, stir-fry garlic and ginger for 30 seconds.
    Add beef, cook until browned.
    Add vegetables, stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
    Serve hot over rice.
