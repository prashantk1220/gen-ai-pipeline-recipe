
Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

    4 bell peppers, halved and seeded
    1 lb ground beef
    1 cup cooked rice
    1 can diced tomatoes
    1 onion, diced
    2 cloves garlic, minced
    1 tsp Italian seasoning
    1 cup shredded cheese

Instructions:

    Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    Brown ground beef with onion and garlic.
    Mix beef with rice, tomatoes, and Italian seasoning.
    Stuff pepper halves with mixture, place in baking dish.
    Cover with foil, bake for 30 minutes. Uncover, top with cheese, bake 10 more minutes.
