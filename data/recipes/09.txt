
Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:

    1 cup Arborio rice
    4 cups vegetable broth, warm
    1 cup mushrooms, sliced
    1/2 cup onion, diced
    2 tbsp butter
    1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

    Saut√© onion and mushrooms in butter until softened.
    Add rice, stir to coat with butter.
    Gradually add broth, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring constantly until absorbed.
    Continue until rice is creamy and cooked (about 20 minutes).
    Stir in Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Serve hot.
