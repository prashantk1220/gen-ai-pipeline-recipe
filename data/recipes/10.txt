
Greek Salad

Ingredients:

    2 cucumbers, chopped
    4 tomatoes, chopped
    1 red onion, sliced
    1 cup Kalamata olives
    200g feta cheese, cubed
    1/4 cup olive oil
    2 tbsp red wine vinegar
    1 tsp dried oregano

Instructions:

    Combine cucumbers, tomatoes, onion, olives, and feta in a large bowl.
    Whisk olive oil, vinegar, and oregano in a small bowl.
    Pour dressing over salad and toss gently.
    Serve immediately or chill before serving.
