
Vegetable Frittata

Ingredients:

    6 eggs
    1/4 cup milk
    1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., spinach, bell peppers, onions)
    1/2 cup grated cheese
    2 tbsp olive oil
    Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

    Whisk eggs and milk in a bowl, add salt and pepper.
    Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet, sautÃ© vegetables until tender.
    Pour egg mixture over vegetables, cook until edges set.
    Sprinkle cheese on top, transfer to preheated broiler.
    Broil for 2-3 minutes until top is golden and puffy.
