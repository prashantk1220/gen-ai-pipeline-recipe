
Vegetarian Chili

Ingredients:

    2 cans kidney beans, drained
    1 can diced tomatoes
    1 onion, chopped
    1 bell pepper, chopped
    2 cloves garlic, minced
    2 tbsp chili powder
    1 tsp cumin
    Salt to taste

Instructions:

    Saut√© onion, bell pepper, and garlic in a pot until softened.
    Add beans, tomatoes, chili powder, and cumin.
    Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    Season with salt to taste.
    Serve hot, optionally with sour cream and cheese.
