
Quinoa Black Bean Salad

Ingredients:

    1 cup quinoa, cooked
    1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
    1 red bell pepper, diced
    1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
    2 tbsp olive oil
    2 tbsp lime juice
    1 tsp cumin
    Salt to taste

Instructions:

    Mix cooked quinoa, black beans, bell pepper, and onion in a bowl.
    Whisk olive oil, lime juice, cumin, and salt in a separate bowl.
    Pour dressing over quinoa mixture and toss well.
    Chill for 30 minutes before serving.
