Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken

Ingredients:

    1 whole chicken (3-4 lbs)
    2 lemons, quartered
    4 cloves garlic, crushed
    2 tbsp olive oil
    1 tbsp dried herbs (rosemary, thyme, oregano)
    Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

    Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
    Mix olive oil, herbs, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
    Rub mixture all over chicken and under skin.
    Stuff cavity with lemon quarters and garlic.
    Roast for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes, until juices run clear.
    Let rest for 10 minutes before carving.
