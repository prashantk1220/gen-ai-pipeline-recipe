
Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:

    1 cup all-purpose flour
    1 tbsp sugar
    2 tsp baking powder
    1 egg
    1 cup milk
    2 ripe bananas, mashed
    2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:

    Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
    In another bowl, whisk egg, milk, mashed bananas, and melted butter.
    Combine wet and dry ingredients, mix until just blended.
    Heat a griddle, pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
    Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook other side until golden.
