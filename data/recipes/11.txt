
Teriyaki Chicken

Ingredients:

    4 chicken thighs
    1/4 cup soy sauce
    2 tbsp brown sugar
    2 tbsp mirin
    1 tbsp grated ginger
    2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

    Mix soy sauce, brown sugar, mirin, ginger, and garlic in a bowl.
    Marinate chicken in the mixture for 30 minutes.
    Preheat grill or pan over medium heat.
    Cook chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until done.
    Brush with remaining marinade while cooking.
