Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken

Ingredients:

    2 chicken breasts
    2 cups fresh spinach
    1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
    2 tbsp olive oil
    1 tsp dried oregano
    Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

    Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    Cut a pocket in each chicken breast.
    Mix spinach, feta, and oregano. Stuff into chicken pockets.
    Season chicken with salt and pepper.
    Heat oil in an oven-safe skillet, sear chicken for 3 minutes per side.
    Transfer skillet to oven, bake for 15-20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
